Silent Guest

In this 3D horror game you are a phrogger. You live in someone’s house without their permission! To survive, you need food, water, and other necessities, but you must retrieve it without the homeowner’s knowledge. Though as you continue to live anonymously in the house, you hear strange noises but think nothing of it. Until one fateful night you saw something you shouldn’t… Now your goal is to escape and not end up as the homeowner's next victim.
In this project I acted as the lead designer and 3D artist of the game. I was in charge of level and narrative design as well as creating assets and laying out the environment of the game.
